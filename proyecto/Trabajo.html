    Greece offers an extraordinary variety of natural landscapes. Dramatic mountains like Mount Olympus and the Pindus range contrast with deep gorges such as Vikos and Samaria. Unique rock formations appear at Meteora, where monasteries sit atop towering pillars, and in volcanic areas like Santorini, Nisyros, and Milos.

    The country is also famous for its crystal-clear waters and striking beaches, including Navagio (Zakynthos), Myrtos (Kefalonia), Porto Katsiki (Lefkada), and the pink sands of Elafonissi (Crete). Caves and underground wonders like Diros Caves and Kleftiko sea caves add to the diversity.
